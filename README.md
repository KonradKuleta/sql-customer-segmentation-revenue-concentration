# SQL Case Study: VIP Customers & Revenue Concentration (SQLite + pandas)

**Scenario:** Synthetic e-commerce dataset (CSV extracts) loaded into a local SQLite database for analysis.

**Objective:** Demonstrate advanced SQL (CTEs + window functions) by answering business-style questions about revenue concentration and VIP customers.

---

## Key questions answered

1. **80/20 check:** How many customers generate ~80% of total revenue?
2. **Revenue concentration:** What share of revenue is generated by the **Top 1% / Top 5% / Top 10%** customers?
3. **VIP list:** Who are the **Top 20 customers**, and what cumulative revenue share do they represent?

---

- **`notebooks/sql_vip_customer.ipynb`** – main notebook (data load → SQLite → SQL queries → results)
